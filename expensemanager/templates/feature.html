Set financial goals to help you save money and achieve your targets. Whether it's saving for a vacation, a new car, or retirement, our Expense Manager makes it easy to track your progress towards your goals.

Keep track of your total expenses over time. Our Expense Manager calculates and displays the total amount of expenses you've added, helping you understand your spending habits and make informed financial decisions.
